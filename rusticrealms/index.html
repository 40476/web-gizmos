How to Play

  • Move with WASD.
  • Left Click to Attack, Gather, or Place objects.
  • Right Click to cancel building.
  • Doors automatically break and snap into walls when clicked on a wall edge.
  • Torches and Campfires provide light at night.
  • Zombies and Ghosts spawn at night. Slimes spawn anytime.
