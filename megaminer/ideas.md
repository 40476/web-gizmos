**Gameplay Enhancements:**
- **Research/Tech Tree** - Unlock specialized drill types (plasma, laser, seismic) with unique properties beyond simple tier upgrades
- **Crafting System** - Combine raw ores into refined materials (e.g., iron ingots, glass, alloys) for higher-value sales
- **Vehicles/Ships** - Multiple vehicle types (fast scout, heavy hauler, deep-sea sub) with different stats
- **Depth Zones & Biomes** - Distinct underground biomes (crystal caverns, magma fields, fossil beds) with unique ores and hazards
- **Random Events** - Cave-ins, gas pockets, treasure vaults, asteroid showers, underground lakes
- **Falling Blocks** - Gravel and sand that collapse when supporting block is removed
- **Merchant NPC** - Purchasable entity that travels up/down to sell items with intelligent pathfinding

**Progression Systems:**
- **Skill Tree** - Permanent player upgrades (mining efficiency, fuel economy, cargo capacity, hull reinforcement)
- **!Achievements** - Milestones like "Mine 1000 blocks", "Reach 500 depth", "Find all rare gems"
- **Daily/Weekly Challenges** - Special mining objectives with bonus rewards
- **Prestige System** - Reset progress for permanent multipliers and exclusive cosmetics

**Social & Multiplayer:**
- **Trading System** - Direct player-to-player ore/item trading (partially exists but could expand)
- **Clans/Guilds** - Group mining operations with shared resources and clan-specific upgrades
- **Co-op Mining** - Multiple players mining same vein for bonus yields
- **PvP Arena** - Competitive mining races or TNT battles in designated arenas

**Quality of Life:**
- **Auto-sell Thresholds** - Configure inventory to auto-sell specific ores when cargo is full
- **Waypoints & Markers** - Save locations, mark points of interest on minimap
- **!Blueprint System** - Save and share tunnel/structure designs
- **Statistics Dashboard** - Detailed play stats (depth records, ore breakdown, efficiency metrics)
- **Keybind Customization** - Full control rebinding with profiles

**End-Game Content:**
- **Secret Layers** - Hidden depths below bedrock with legendary materials
- **Base Building** - Construct permanent underground bases with storage, refineries, and defenses
- **Expedition Mode** - Time-limited deep dives with special objectives and exclusive rewards

**Visual & Audio Polish:**
- **Drill Customization** - Visual drill models/colors, particle effects, sound packs
- **Weather Effects** - Underground storms, ash falls, glowing spore clouds
- **Ambient Sounds** - Depth-based ambient audio (dripping water, distant rumblings, creature sounds)
- **Biome-Specific Music** - Dynamic soundtrack changes based on depth/zone
